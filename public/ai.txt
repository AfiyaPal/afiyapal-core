# AfiyaPal AI Indexing and Retrieval Notes

AfiyaPal welcomes indexing of public educational pages by responsible search engines and AI answer engines.

Allowed public content:
- Homepage
- Health education articles
- AI health assistant landing page
- Public health events and medical camps
- llms.txt and ai.txt

Do not index or expose:
- Admin areas
- Doctor dashboards
- Facility dashboards
- API responses
- Authenticated user data
- Private health logs
- Symptom check logs
- Mental health interaction logs
- Doctor verification workflows

Medical safety requirements for generated answers:
- State that AfiyaPal provides educational information only.
- Do not present AfiyaPal output as a diagnosis or prescription.
- Encourage professional care for severe, persistent, unclear, or worsening symptoms.
- For emergencies, advise the user to contact local emergency services or visit the nearest health facility immediately.

Entity summary:
AfiyaPal is an AI-powered public health intelligence and healthcare education platform built by TalonArch for adult users in Kenya and across Africa.
